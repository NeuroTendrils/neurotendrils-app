# EEG replay data attribution

The motor-imagery stream and labels under this directory are derived from
BNCI2014-001 (BCI Competition IV Dataset 2a): four-class cue-based motor
imagery (left hand, right hand, both feet, tongue), 22 EEG channels @ 250 Hz.

## Dataset identity

- Code: BNCI2014-001
- Also known as: BCI Competition IV Dataset 2a
- Catalog: BNCI Horizon 2020 / BBCI Competition IV
- https://www.bnci-horizon-2020.eu/database/data-sets
- https://www.bbci.de/competition/iv/
- https://www.bbci.de/competition/iv/desc_2a.pdf

NeuroTendrils ships a processed export (ONNX model + continuous float32 stream)
for offline demonstration. Cite the paper above when referring to the underlying
EEG data.
